osulist is an online web application that lets you easily create and share playlists of individual osu! beatmap difficulties. If you have any bugs you would like to report, or if you would like to help contribute to the development of osulist, join our Discord server!

To create a new playlist, login using the 'login' button on the top right of the website. Once logged in, click on 'new playlist' on the navigation bar.
